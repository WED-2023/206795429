    About Me


    My father was a kind, smart and generous man.
    His both parents immigrated to Israel on 1951 from Iraq.
    My father was born on 1959. He studied in "Beit Yaacov" school in Rehovot.
    He served the Israeli Air Force as an airplane technician.
    After he finished his service he started working in "Tenuva" dairy in Rehovot.
    He married my mom Rachel on 1991 and my older sister was born soon after on 1992.
    We are 3 sisters. My older sister also married and gave birth to my beautiful niece that my father loved more than ever.
    On September 2021 my father was diagnosed with cancer, and after a prolonged battle with the disease,
    he passed away last December.
